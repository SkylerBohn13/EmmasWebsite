Purpose for the Concerto Pro app:

All around the world there are many, many young pianists that are struggling with the same problem that I have been, the problem of having to practice concertos with a second piano.

My app concerto pro fixes this problem by providing piano players like me, with a second piano to practice with! Concerto pro not only functions as a double for the second piano but can also double for the first piano to make practice more simple.

Concerto Pro is now published in both the app store and google play. I also created the app face design myself starting with a draft on paper and then importing it on to adobe to create the final result!
